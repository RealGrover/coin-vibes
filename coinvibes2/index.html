About Coin Vibes

Coin Vibes is an artistic exploration of Bitcoin's new unit standard. We're vibecoding creative UI examples that show how the Coin Standard (1 BTC = 100,000,000¢) makes Bitcoin feel like real money again.

No more confusing decimals. No more "sats" that sound like made-up internet points. Just intuitive, monetary-friendly displays that humans actually understand.

The Coin Standard BIP

The Coin Standard is a Bitcoin Improvement Proposal that redefines the base unit from "satoshi" to "coin" with the ¢ symbol.
